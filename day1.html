Day 1: Mathematics for AI

There are two parts to Day 1: Fundamentals of Linear Algebra (morning) and Fundamentals of Calculus (afternoon). In Linear Algebra, we cover vectors and their linear combinations, the dot product, systems of linear equations and orthogonal projections onto a line or onto a plane. In Calculus, we review elementary functions and their composition, the notion of derivative, as well as rules of differentiation, and linear and quadratic approximations of a function at a point. We wrap up with the discussion of the behavior of the function from its derivative.
